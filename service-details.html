Penetration Testing

Join our elite team of ethical hackers and security professionals, specializing in the art of penetration testing. As a Penetration Testing Specialist at Secure Cyber Future you will be at the helm of our security assessments, delving deep into networks and systems to identify and exploit vulnerabilities. Your mission? To simulate real-world attacks, fortify defenses, and ensure our clients are impenetrable against cyber threats.

Responsibilities

- Conduct comprehensive penetration tests on client systems, networks, and applications.
- Identify and exploit security vulnerabilities to demonstrate potential real-world risks.
- Collaborate with our clients to provide detailed reports and actionable recommendations for remediation.
- Stay ahead of emerging threats and continuously enhance our penetration testing methodologies.


Qualifications

- Proficiency in industry-standard penetration testing tools such as Metasploit, Nmap, Burp Suite, etc.
- In-depth knowledge of network protocols, operating systems, and cybersecurity best practices.
- Certifications such as CEH, OSCP, or equivalent are highly valued.
- Strong analytical skills and the ability to think like a hacker to anticipate potential exploits.
